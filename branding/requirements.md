# Harbor Roast Requirements and Branding

## Business Snapshot

- Name: Harbor Roast
- Positioning: Neighborhood coffee shop serving espresso, slow bar coffee, and fresh pastries in a warm coastal-inspired setting
- Audience: Morning commuters, remote workers, weekend brunch visitors, and local residents
- Brand personality: Welcoming, grounded, craft-focused, calm, and modern

## Logo Direction

- Primary mark: A circular badge with a coffee cup, rising steam, and a sun arc to suggest warmth and an early-morning ritual
- Wordmark: `Harbor Roast`
- Style: Clean, minimal, and usable across signage, menus, cups, and social avatars
- Starter asset in repo: `branding/logo.svg`

## Color Preferences

- Espresso: `#4B3326`
- Cream: `#F6EFE7`
- Terracotta: `#C86A4A`
- Sage: `#7A8B72`
- Charcoal: `#2B2521`

These colors support a cozy coffee identity without leaning too dark or overly rustic.

## Core Copy

- Tagline: `Small-batch coffee for slow mornings.`
- Short description: `Harbor Roast is a neighborhood coffee shop built around careful espresso, seasonal drinks, and a room that invites people to stay a little longer.`
- About copy: `We serve balanced espresso, rotating single-origin coffees, and a focused menu of pastries and toasts. The space should feel warm, calm, and easy to return to every day.`
- CTA copy: `Order Ahead`, `View Menu`, `Visit the Cafe`

## Image Direction

- Hero image: Natural-light photo of espresso drinks and pastries on a warm wood table
- Secondary interior image: Calm cafe seating with plants, ceramic cups, and soft morning light
- Product images: Close-up shots of latte art, pour-over service, pastries, and signature seasonal drinks
- Team image: Barista preparing espresso behind the bar
- Style notes: Warm tones, real textures, candid moments, and no overly staged stock imagery

## Menu Highlights

- Harbor Latte: House espresso, steamed milk, and brown sugar sea salt syrup
- Maple Oat Cold Brew: Cold brew with maple, oat milk, and cinnamon
- Sunrise Pour Over: Rotating single-origin filter coffee
- Rosemary Mocha: Dark chocolate mocha with rosemary-infused syrup
- Butter Croissant: Daily baked flaky pastry
- Citrus Ricotta Toast: Sourdough toast with whipped ricotta, orange zest, and honey

## Recommended Usage

- Use cream backgrounds with espresso or charcoal text for primary readability
- Reserve terracotta for buttons, accents, and featured menu callouts
- Use sage sparingly for secondary accents and environmental details
- Keep copy concise and warm, with emphasis on craft and routine
